# **App Name**: Interview Ace

## Core Features:

- Customizable Interview Simulation: Allow users to select the job role and seniority level to tailor the interview questions to specific roles and experience levels.
- AI Question Generation: Use a large language model to generate interview questions dynamically based on the selected role and seniority, creating a unique interview experience each time.
- Voice Analysis and Feedback: Analyze the user's voice during the interview, providing feedback on tone, clarity, and pace.
- Behavioral Analysis Tool: Employ a tool powered by a large language model to evaluate interview responses for sentiment and behavioral cues, providing feedback on the candidate's approach and communication style.
- Progress Tracking: Enable users to track their progress over time, viewing metrics such as question response quality and overall interview scores.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep indigo (#4B0082) to evoke professionalism and confidence.
- Background color: Very light indigo (#F0E6FF) to provide a calm, neutral backdrop.
- Accent color: Lavender (#E6E6FA) for interactive elements, ensuring clear visual cues.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif.
- Use sharp, vector-based icons to represent job roles and feedback categories.
- Design a clean, structured layout with clear sections for interview questions, feedback, and progress tracking.
- Incorporate subtle animations for loading screens and feedback displays, maintaining a professional and engaging experience.
